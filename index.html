About Varalaxmi Homemade

Founded in 2010 in the picturesque hills of Coorg, Varalaxmi Homemade began as a small family kitchen creating chocolates, spices, and wines for local communities.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local business as word spread about our authentic flavors and traditional preparation methods.

Today, we continue to handcraft each product with the same love and attention to detail, using only the finest locally sourced ingredients from Coorg's fertile lands.
